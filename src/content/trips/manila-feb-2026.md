Four days in Metro Manila. Day one was the Friday check-in at Acqua
Private Residences in Mandaluyong, with a coffee stop at Riverwalk Cafe
nearby. Saturday packed in the Filipina Meet app launch at Rockwell
Center, a UAAP game between De La Salle and FEU, and dinner at Pepper
Lunch in MOA. Sunday was the 3D trick-art museum Art in Island, and
Monday wrapped up with a Mary Grace meal before the trip home.
