Digital Freelancer Profiling App

Overview:
The Digital Freelancer Profiling System (DFPS) is a web-based platform for registering, verifying, and managing digital freelancers in General Santos City. It provides CEMCDO with data-driven tools for policy formulation and workforce planning.

Collaborators:
BSIT students of Mindanao State University – General Santos City with guidance from Dr. Lumer Jude P. Doce and support from the City Economic Management and Cooperative Development Office (CEMCDO).

Introduction

The rise of digital freelancers in General Santos City offers economic opportunities but poses challenges for governance. LGU initiatives have supported freelancers through upskilling and networking, yet lack a centralized system to track demographics, specializations, and needs, often leaving policy decisions based on incomplete data.

The DFPS addresses this gap by serving as a digital repository where freelancers build profiles, upload portfolios, and access support services. For the LGU, it provides dashboards and analytics for evidence-based decision-making.

Tech Stack

Component Technology
Frontend HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript
Backend Python, Django
Database SQLite
Deployment PythonAnywhere
Documentation MS Word (APA 7), Draw.io for diagrams

Objectives

  • Develop a profiling system that allows freelancers to register, update, and manage their profiles.
  • Provide CEMCDO with a centralized, real-time database of freelancers for planning and verification.
  • Support data-driven policy creation and strategic initiatives for digital workforce development.
  • Facilitate employer access to a vetted pool of freelancers categorized by skill, specialization, and experience.

Features

  • Freelancer Registration and Login: Secure authentication and onboarding.
  • Profile Management: Upload profile pictures, resumes, skills, experiences, and portfolios.
  • Admin Dashboard: Visualizes gender, location, skillset, and platform usage.
  • Certificate Upload: Attach training and qualification certificates.
  • Verification System: CEMCDO reviews and verifies freelancer profiles.
  • Announcements and Events Module: Posts digital economy events and trainings.
  • Skill and Experience Analytics: Graphs and tables showing distributions by specialization, barangay, gender, and age group.
