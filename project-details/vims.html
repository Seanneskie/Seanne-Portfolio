Overview:
Vessel Inventory Management System designed for TSP Marine Industries, providing a role-based web interface to streamline tracking, transfers, requests, and adjustments of vessel stocks.

Collaborators:
Developed in collaboration with the MIS of TSP Marine Industries.

Introduction

VIMS centralizes vessel supply management for TSP Marine Industries, offering crews and administrators a unified portal to monitor stock levels, request items, and oversee transfers across departments and fleets.

Details

Built with modern web technologies, the system keeps comprehensive logs of consumption and adjustments while synchronizing data through Supabase for reliability. Reports can be exported to Excel or CSV, and action prompts confirm critical inventory operations.

Features

  • Inventory dashboard to view stock levels for each vessel
  • Report generation with export to Excel/CSV formats
  • Stock transfers between vessels with pending-transfer tracking
  • Vessel management for organizing departments and fleets
  • User management with role-based access control for crew and admins
  • Supply request submission, approval, and tracking workflows
  • Cloud-stored data synchronized through Supabase for reliability
  • Action prompts providing confirmation for critical inventory operations
  • Consumption and adjustment logging to record stock usage and corrections
