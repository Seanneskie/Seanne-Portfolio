Overview:
A comprehensive fund utilization and cooperative profiling system for the City Economic Management and Cooperative Development Office of General Santos City.

Collaborators:
Azlan Tomindug, Kimberly Baylon, Bridget Jose, Francheska Besana, Seanne Cañete

Project Overview

The CEMCDO web application supports cooperative databanking, division–level financial management and fund augmentation with role–based access.

  • Transparent tracking of budgets, expenses and reports
  • Management of obligation and purchase requests
  • Fund transfers, amendments and augmentations
  • Profiling of cooperatives with asset and product tracking
  • Dashboards for administrators, officers and cooperatives

Core Modules

Authentication & User Roles

  • Super Admin manages accounts, roles and logs
  • Division officers handle their financial tools
  • Cooperatives view profiles, assets and reports

Fund Utilization

  • Dashboard of accounts, budgets and charts
  • Division account budgeting and performance
  • Transaction tracking for income and expenses
  • Appropriations and year–wise allocations
  • Fund augmentations and amendments
  • Excel or PDF reporting

Obligation & Purchase Requests

  • Register obligations with payee and documents
  • Purchase requests with itemization and attachments
  • Approval flow and exportable records

Cooperative Profiling

Super Admin

  • Manages reference data and coop accounts
  • CRUD on geographic data

Coop Officer

  • Dashboard with map view and notifications
  • Profile management and asset tracking
  • Generate reports and manage members

Reports & Analytics

  • Export Excel reports for funds and coop data
  • Visualization of budgets and transaction trends
